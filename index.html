What Can You Do With Stymied Golf?

Connect Locally

Gone are the days of being paired up with a random group at the golf course! Stymied Golf allows you to connect with other golfers in your area. We use your approximate location to connect you with golfers in a radius of your choosing.

Play With Golfers of Your Skill Level

Going out on the golf course can be intimidating when you’re just beginning. You have the option to input your handicap while creating your profile. This will allow you to find golfers of similar skill levels in your area to connect with.

Find & Create Games to Play In

Find and create games to play in. Once you’re apart of the Stymied Golf community, you’ll be able to reach out to join others requests to play, or create your own game for others to join.

Travel for Work?

Traveling for work? When you land in a new city, jump on Stymied Golf to get some course suggestions from the locals. Or better yet, set up a game with someone who can show you around. Maybe you’ll even get a few members bounces!

Rate & Review

Rate and review golf courses in your area. Keep the local golfers in your area in the know about who has the purest greens, who just plugged, pace of play etc.

Marketplace

Let’s face it, we all are searching for that perfect golf club. That means sometimes we have to make some more room in our bag. Stymied Golf has a marketplace specifically for that reason. Buy or sell new and used equipment from the Stymied Golf community.
